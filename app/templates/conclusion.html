Conclusion

Importance

The project conducted here has implications regarding team composition analysis for IPL teams going forward. The best teams have historically been those to find their footing and best playing XI earlier into the tournament, and undoubtedly, part of that is choosing favorable matchups between batsmen and bowlers.

Future Analysis

Unfortunately, I did not get to pursue as much of the paths this project could go as I would like. A few of the areas I would like to take this would be:

  • Changing this to a classification problem
    • Define new "classes" of players
    • New metrics like F1 score, accuracy, etc.
  • Predict match results by change in extraneous variables
    • Would a match won in 2014 be won in 2007?
    • How would the 2020 season have turned out if it was played in a different country?
  • Application to non-IPL data
    • Obtain data on international matches / other formats
    • Do these have the same patterns as IPL matches?
